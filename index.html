Recipes

Ingredients

Cookie

  • 420 grams of flour
  • 330 grams brown sugar
  • 225 grams butter
  • 10 grams vanilla
  • 06 grams salt
  • 05 grams baking soda
  • 03 grams cinnamon powder
  • 02 eggs

Glazed

  • 100 grams butter
  • 100 grams of cream cheese
  • 40 grams brown suggar

Procedure

    • Add butter to a bowl and knead briefly
    • Add the eggs 1 by 1 to the mixture until it is combined.
    • Add dry ingredients previously passed through a strainer
    • If required, add sprinkles, coloring or seeds to taste.
    • Divide the mixture into 50 gram spheres.
    • Preheat the oven to 200 grams for ten minutes, then transfer the cookies to the oven
      and bake for ten minutes.
